Burlington Free Meals Program

Who We Are

My name is Nick Gambill and I founded Salvation Suppers in 2020 in partnership with the Salvation Army of Burlington's free meals program. Salvation Supper's goal is to involve local cooks, chefs, and food suppliers in a communal effort to give back to the community. Since its formation, Salvation Suppers has served hundreds of meals while establishing partnerships with local businesses such as Brio Coffee and Zero Gravity Beerhall. Salvation Suppers is focused on bridging the gap between mutual aid efforts and Burlington's skilled creatives.
